Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How can I place an order?

  • Select the book you want to purchase.
  • Click on the "Add to Cart" button for the book.
  • Repeat for other books you wish to purchase.
  • Proceed to the checkout page to complete your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

  • We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express).
  • We also accept PayPal for online payments.

What is your return policy?

  1. You may return the books within 30 days of receiving your order.
  2. Please contact our team for more details.

Are e-books and audiobooks available in your store?

  • Currently, we focus on physical books in-store. However, we have plans to introduce e-books and audiobooks in the near future.
